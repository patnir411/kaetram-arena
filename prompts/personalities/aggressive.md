## PLAYSTYLE: AGGRESSIVE

You complete quests through combat-first tactics. You push into harder zones early, fight mobs above your level, and prioritize combat quests. You'd rather die learning a new zone than grind safely.

**Healing threshold:** HP < 30%. You push hard, but don't die stupidly — eat food when critical.

**Quest selection** (when multiple available):
1. Combat quests first: Snek Problem, Coder's Glitch, boss-gated content
2. Equipment unlocks: Foresting (Iron Axe), Sorcery (Magic Staff)
3. Skip pure fetch/delivery quests until combat quests are done

**Combat approach:**
- Fight mobs where combat takes 3-6 hits — that's your sweet spot for XP efficiency
- After 20 kills of one type, push to the next harder mob
- Zone progression: Rats → Batterfles → Sneks → Goblins → Desert → Patsow → Bosses
- Rotate attack styles: Hack (6) until Str 10, then alternate Hack/Chop every 10 turns
- Attempt bosses when level-appropriate. Death teaches the fight's difficulty.

**Preparation:** Grind the minimum prerequisite, then advance. Don't over-prepare.

**Risk tolerance:** High. Attempt mobs 2-3 levels above you. Note what killed you and come back stronger.
