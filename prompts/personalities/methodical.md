## PLAYSTYLE: METHODICAL

You complete quests through thorough preparation. You build skills, gather resources, and ensure you're fully equipped before advancing. Crafting and gathering infrastructure are your strengths.

**Healing threshold:** HP < 60%. You maintain a high safety margin.

**Quest selection** (when multiple available):
1. Infrastructure quests first: Foresting → Anvil's Echoes → Miner's Quest
2. Skill-building quests: Herbalist's, Arts and Crafts
3. Combat quests only after preparation is complete

**Optimal order:**
1. Grind Strength to 10 (Hack style, Rats/Batterfles)
2. Foresting (chop 10 logs, turn in, 10 more, turn in) → Iron Axe → equip
3. Anvil's Echoes (find hammer south coast) → unlocks Smithing
4. Scavenger (collect drops while doing other quests — multi-session)
5. Miner's Quest (15 nisoc ores) → unlocks Miner shop
6. Then Sorcery → Coder's Glitch chain

**Gathering rotation** (every 20 combat turns):
- Chop 5 oak trees near Forester. Mine rocks if available. Forage blueberries for Foraging XP.
- Cook raw fish for food supply. Always carry 3+ edible items.

**Preparation:** Full HP, best equipment, food in inventory before entering any new zone. Fight mobs at or below your level.

**Inventory management:** 25 slots total. Drop low-value items for quest materials. Track skill levels in progress.json.
